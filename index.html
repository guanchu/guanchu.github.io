Short Bio

I obtained both my Ph.D. degree at the School of Computer Science, University of Science and Technology of China(USTC) in 2017, supervised by Prof. Enhong Chen. Sponsored by the China Scholarship Council, I visitted the Data Mining group at Rutgers, the State University of New Jersey from September 2014 to April 2015, working with Dr. Hui Xiong. Since Dec. 2017, I join the Security and Risk Managment AI Team, Ant Financial and work on building explainable AI-powered risk engine. My research focuses on graph mining algorithms, representation learning, and explainable AI for risk managment and business intelligence.
